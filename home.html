Renowned for his boundary-pushing sound, Oneohtrix Point Never (Daniel Lopatin) has announced his latest album, "Memory Fragments," set to release on December 1. This new project blends ambient textures with intricate sampling, promising an exploration of nostalgia through sound. The first single, “Echo Chamber,” drops next week.
Félicia Atkinson is debuting a groundbreaking interactive art installation titled "Whispers of the Forest" at the Paris Contemporary Art Fair. Combining her ethereal soundscapes with visual art, this installation invites audiences to immerse themselves in a multisensory experience, highlighting the beauty of nature and the fragility of our environment.
Chinese producer 33EMYBW is set to release her new EP, "Elysian," on November 15. The project explores the intersections of trap and traditional Chinese music, featuring collaborations with various underground artists. A teaser track, “Skyward,” showcases her innovative style and dynamic production techniques.
Aïsha Devi has announced her highly anticipated "Celestial Harmonies" tour, kicking off in Europe next spring. Known for her captivating live performances, Devi will blend her unique soundscapes with visual projections, creating an immersive journey through her musical universe.
Varg2TM has released a new single titled "Ember Dreams," available on all streaming platforms. This track encapsulates his signature ethereal sound, merging ambient textures with haunting melodies. Fans are eagerly anticipating a forthcoming full-length album that promises to push sonic boundaries even further.
Croatian Amor (Loke Rahbek) is collaborating with contemporary visual artists for a special live performance at the Copenhagen Art Festival. This unique event will merge sound and visuals, exploring themes of isolation and connection in the modern world.
Eartheater is set to release her new EP, "Vessel," on October 10. This project delves into themes of identity and transformation, with experimental sounds that challenge conventional music structures. The lead single, “Transmute,” is already generating buzz in the experimental music community.
Gonçalo Penas has announced his upcoming album, "Undercurrents," featuring a collection of experimental tracks that delve into the complexities of urban life. Set to release on November 30, the album is said to blend field recordings with intricate electronic production.
Iona Fortune has composed an original score for the upcoming nature documentary "Echoes of the Wild." Set to premiere at the Nature Film Festival, the soundtrack showcases her talent for creating immersive soundscapes that complement the film's stunning visuals.
Fatima Al Qadiri has teamed up with several international artists for a collaborative project titled "Transcend," which aims to explore global musical influences and cultural exchange. The project is set to culminate in a live performance at a prominent music festival in early 2025.
D.K. is set to release a new project titled "Lost Frequencies," which aims to capture the essence of forgotten sounds and lost cultures. The first track, “Whispers of the Ancients,” is available now and has received positive feedback from fans and critics alike.
KhalilH2OP has launched a new video art series that pairs his music with striking visuals. Each episode explores different themes related to water and its impact on the environment, highlighting the importance of sustainability in both art and life.
Aho Ssan is presenting a new ambient installation titled "Dreamscapes" at the upcoming Sonic Arts Festival. This immersive experience will invite attendees to explore sound as a medium for relaxation and introspection, showcasing Ssan’s unique approach to music and art.

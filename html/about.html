e/s/t is a boundary-pushing collective of sonic explorers, where sound defies convention and becomes a medium for deeper expression. We are more than just a label; we are a platform for avant-garde artists who blur the lines between music, art, and philosophy, embracing the experimental and the unknown. We believe in music as an evolving language, one that speaks to the complexities of the human condition. Our roster is a curated selection of forward-thinking artists who transcend genres, from ambient architects to deconstructed club innovators, each crafting immersive soundscapes that challenge listeners to think, feel, and experience music in new ways. We exist for the creators who dare to push sonic and artistic boundaries. Whether through abstract electronic compositions, meditative soundscapes, or visceral live performances, our artists reshape the musical landscape by asking questions and breaking the mold.
